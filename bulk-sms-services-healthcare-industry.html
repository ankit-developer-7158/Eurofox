Bulk SMS Services for Healthcare Industry

Bulk SMS service has made its strong place in hospitals and other healthcare organisations. Doctors are preferring SMS marketing to send any kind of information to their patients. SMS marketing is very cost effective technique to establish connection with the customers or patients.

Bulk SMS has made it possible for the healthcare industry to have a direct reach within the patients and public in a well organised manner. This has reduced the unwanted crowd of people waiting for their medical reports or fixing appointments.

How to Use Bulk SMS Service for Hospitals?

    (Healthcare industry can use Bulk SMS service in many ways to provide smooth service. Here are the ways:)

  •   Send Appointment Reminders via SMS

    • You can send reminder messages regarding the appointments to your patients quickly. Send updates of the appointments like cancellation, next date and time for appointment, waiting number etc. Patients find these SMSs very useful.

  •   Send Medical Alerts

    • You can send alert messages to the patients and staff members about any emergency instantly. It is the most effective way of transferring information than any other method.

  •   Send Health Reports

    • Doctors and healthcare industry are widely using Bulk SMS service to send medical reports to their patients. This has made easy for the patients to get the test results on their phones without rushing to the hospital.

  •   Spread Health Awareness

    • Create messages having information on how to keep yourself healthy, tips, do’s and don’ts etc. This will raise your image in your patients and they will be more loyal to you and your organisation.

  •   Encourage Donations

    • Bulk SMS service lets you create and send messages asking for donations for your organisation. It inspires the people to do the noble work and motivate others to donate

  •   Encourage Regular Check-Ups

    • Send messages to encourage the routine check-up of health in the public. Create messages having details of need of check-up, dangerous effects of irregular check-ups etc. This will not only make the people understand the need of regular health check-up but also inspire them to get checked-up by the doctors

Advantages of Bulk SMS Services in Healthcare Industry

    (Bulk SMS offers many benefits to the healthcare sector in easy way. Below is the list of advantages of using bulk SMS service in healthcare organisations:)

  •   Get SMS Templates for Different Purpose

    • Bulk SMS service provides different templates for creating unique messages. You just have to modify these templates as per your need. Modification of these templates are very easy

  •   Establish Bond with Your Patients

    • Sending Personalised messages to the patients makes them feel special. This creates a special place for your organisation

  •   Raise Organisation’s Value

    • Sending regular messages to the patients leads to increased loyalty for you resulting into increased brand value.

  •   Boost Productivity

    • SMS marketing makes the customer support very smooth and better leading to high productivity

  •   High Customer Satisfaction

    • Patients are getting every information from your organisation through the SMS. This reduces them to make unnecessary efforts to go to the hospitals.

  •   Promote New Branch of Your Hospital

    • Send promotional messages to the public informing them about the location of your new branch. You need not to waste money on distributing pamphlets.
